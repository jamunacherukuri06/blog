About Manali

Manali is a charming hill station nestled in the mountains of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Situated at an altitude of 2050 meters above sea level, Manali is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and gushing rivers. It offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and honeymooners alike.

Ideal Duration: 3 to 4 Days

Nearest City: Kullu

Distance From Hyderabad: 2045 Kms

Peak Season: March to June, October to February

State: Himachal Pradesh
