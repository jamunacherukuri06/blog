About Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal, often referred to as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a picturesque hill station located in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Situated in the Palani Hills of the Western Ghats, Kodaikanal is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, serene lakes, lush greenery, and pleasant climate. It is a popular tourist destination and offers various attractions such as boating, trekking, and visiting scenic viewpoints.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Madurai

Distance From Hyderabad: 976 Kms

Peak Season: April to June, September to October

State: Tamil Nadu
