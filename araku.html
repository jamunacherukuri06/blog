About Araku Valley

Araku Valley is located in the Vishakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh, India. Surrounded by the magnificent Eastern Ghats, the valley offers refreshing breaks for nature lovers. It is known for its exquisite biodiversity and features lush green tea and coffee plantations.

Ideal Duration: 1 to 2 Days

Nearest City: Vizag or Visakhapatnam

Distance From Hyderabad: 650 Kms

Peak Season: September to May

State: Andhra Pradesh
