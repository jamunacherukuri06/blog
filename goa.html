About Goa

Goa is a state located in the western region of India, on the coast of the Arabian Sea. Renowned for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa attracts tourists from all over the world. The state is also known for its delicious cuisine, water sports activities, and historic churches and forts.

Ideal Duration: 4 to 7 Days

Nearest City: Panaji

Distance From Hyderabad: 675 km

Peak Season: October to January

State: Goa
