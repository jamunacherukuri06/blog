About Dharamshala

Dharamshala is a beautiful hill station nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its serene natural beauty, majestic mountains, and Tibetan culture. The town is divided into two parts - Lower Dharamshala, which is the commercial center, and Upper Dharamshala, which is known for its spiritual and cultural significance.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Pathankot

Distance From Hyderabad: 2000 Kms

Peak Season: March to June, September to December

State: Himachal Pradesh
