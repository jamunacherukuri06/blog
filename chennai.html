About Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Situated on the Coromandel Coast off the Bay of Bengal, Chennai is one of the biggest cultural, economic, and educational centers in South India. The city is famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and delicious South Indian cuisine.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Pondicherry

Distance From Hyderabad: 631 Kms

Peak Season: November to February

State: Tamil Nadu
