About Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. Situated on the Deccan Plateau in the southeastern part of Karnataka, Bangalore is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its thriving information technology industry. The city is also renowned for its pleasant climate, beautiful parks, and vibrant nightlife.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Mysore

Distance From Hyderabad: 570 Kms

Peak Season: October to February

State: Karnataka
