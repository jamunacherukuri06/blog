About Mysore

Mysore, officially known as Mysuru, is a city located in the southern part of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is famous for its rich heritage, magnificent palaces, and vibrant culture. Mysore is renowned for its grand Dasara festival celebrations, during which the city comes alive with colorful processions, traditional dances, and music performances.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Bangalore

Distance From Hyderabad: 715 Kms

Peak Season: October to March

State: Karnataka
