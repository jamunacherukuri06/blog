About Ooty

Ooty, officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. It is famous for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, and lush green landscapes. Ooty is a favored destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and honeymooners alike.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Coimbatore

Distance From Hyderabad: 828 Kms

Peak Season: April to June, September to November

State: Tamil Nadu
