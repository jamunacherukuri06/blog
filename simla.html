About Shimla

Shimla is the capital city of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Nestled in the Himalayan foothills, it is known for its picturesque beauty, colonial architecture, and pleasant weather. Shimla offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, lush greenery, and charming streets, making it a popular destination for tourists.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Chandigarh

Distance From Hyderabad: 1870 Kms

Peak Season: March to June, December to January

State: Himachal Pradesh
