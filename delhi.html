About Delhi

Delhi, officially known as the National Capital Territory of Delhi, is the capital city of India. It is a massive metropolitan area situated in the northern part of India. Delhi is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural marvels such as the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. The city is a bustling hub of commerce, politics, and culture.
