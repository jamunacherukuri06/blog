About Jammu

Jammu is the winter capital of the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. It is located in the foothills of the Himalayas and is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic temples, and scenic beauty. Jammu serves as the gateway to the famous Vaishno Devi shrine and is a popular tourist destination for pilgrims and nature lovers alike.

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 Days

Nearest City: Katra

Distance From Hyderabad: 2113 Kms

Peak Season: March to October

Union Territory: Jammu and Kashmir
