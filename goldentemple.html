About the Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a Gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is one of the most revered spiritual sites for Sikhs and one of the most visited religious places in the world. The temple's stunning architecture, surrounded by a sacred pool, attracts pilgrims and tourists alike.

Ideal Duration: 1 Day

Nearest City: Amritsar

Distance From Hyderabad:662 km

Peak Season: October to March

State: Punjab
